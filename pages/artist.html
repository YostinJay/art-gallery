About Xelxa

Xelxa is an incredibly creative person who finds joy in using the game Garry's Mod as a medium for art. Their passion for creating digital art knows no bounds as they use Garry's Mod to create surreal, funny, disturbing, and thought-provoking art pieces. The game allows them to experiment with different elements, characters, and environments, and the results are always impressive. Xelxa's art is a testament to their extraordinary imagination, and their use of Garry's Mod as a canvas proves that creativity knows no bounds.
